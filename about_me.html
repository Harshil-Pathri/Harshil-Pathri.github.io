About Me

A Little Bit About Me

My name is Elizabeth Bennet. I am twenty years old, and am engaged to the brilliant Mr. Darcy. At the moment, I live with my parents in Longbourn, Hertfordshire near Meryton, but soon I plan to move in with Mr. Darcy and his sister at Pemberley.

My Personality

I see myself as quite an intelligent and analytical individual. Though I do not wish to appear as self-conceited, I am, with the possible exception of Jane, the most sensible among my sisters.

My Interests

I enjoy going on long walks through parks and love to see the beauty of nature. Most recently, I visited the amazing gardens of Pemberley, which was extraordinarily beautiful. Another of my pastimes is to analyze people. I often get a very good idea of what someone is truly like, simply by deeply analyzing their behavior, manner of speaking, and occasionally, their countenance.
