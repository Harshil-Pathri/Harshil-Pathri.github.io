The Pride

What is Pride

In the 21st century, the term, pride, is often used with positive connotation. In my times, however, the term pride to being essentially full of yourself or biggety. People who were prideful were considered bad and not worth effort spent to reason with them.

Mr. Darcy's Relationship with Pride

When I first met Mr. Darcy, nearly everyone believed him to be a smug, disagreeable person. He acted as though he was better than, well practically everyone. However, after falling in love with me, his arrogance began to decline and he became a quite pleasant acquantaince.
